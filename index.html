Side Projects

tutel.rocks

I happened upon a pet turtle and I wanted an easier way to check in on him when I'm not around, but the camera app I use is a bit clunky. So I made a website to live stream his antics for my own viewing, and for my friends when they're bored. (Be warned, turtles really don't do a lot...)

I'm working on a way to have this more widely accessible, but the internet is a scary place to have a feed of your turtle for anyone to see. For now, you have to know me to have access.
